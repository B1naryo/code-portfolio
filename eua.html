About

Who am I

My name is Sandro Henrique Cerqueira, I'm 31 years old, and I have a degree in Computer Science. Currently, I live in a small town in the state of São Paulo.

A little of my story


My journey in the world of pentesting began at the age of 18, when I immersed myself in studying Pentest. At 19, I decided to further enhance my knowledge by enrolling in the Computer Science course. It was then, in the year 2023, that I was introduced to the bug bounty world.

I confess that initially, I was completely unaware of this area. Bug bounty, a practice in which renowned companies reward individuals for identifying vulnerabilities in their systems, turned out to be an exciting discovery. Although it is a complex and highly competitive field, I noticed that the number of companies offering bug rewards is relatively small compared to the total number of existing organizations.

While exploring the internet, I found that about 80% of web applications have some kind of vulnerability, from large companies to the smallest ones. This realization was the starting point for my idea of ​​creating this site. My purpose is to identify vulnerabilities in web applications for companies that do not participate in bug bounty programs, with the aim of helping them reinforce the security of their services and protect their customers' data.
